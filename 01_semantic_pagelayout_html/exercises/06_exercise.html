Recipe for Omeletes

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 2 tsp of Oil
  • 1/2 tsp of Salt
  • 1/2 tsp of Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them together.
  2. Chop the tomato, onion, and capsicum into small pieces.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the chopped vegetables to the pan and saut√© them until they are soft.
  5. Pour the whisked eggs into the pan and cook them until they are set.
  6. Season the omelette with salt and black pepper.
  7. Flip the omelette over and cook it for another minute.
  8. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve it hot.
